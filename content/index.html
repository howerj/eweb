eweb server

Great Scot! It works!

eweb is a tiny web-server written in C, with its operating system dependent parts placed behind a series of callbacks. It should be usable within embedded devices that have a TCP/IP stack that acts like the original Berkeley Sockets stack.

The Purpose

To serve web-pages! :O

History

This web-server is heavily based on the dweb web-server, which is in turn based on the nweb web-server. They are both tiny web-servers written in C.
